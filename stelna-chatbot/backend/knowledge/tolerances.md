## General Manufacturing Tolerances

Tolerance requirements depend on the manufacturing process selected.

CNC Machining can achieve tight tolerances typically around ±0.05 mm for precision components.

3D Printing tolerances typically range between ±0.1 mm to ±0.3 mm depending on machine type and material.

Injection Molding can achieve consistent tolerances once tooling is finalized, but part shrinkage must be considered in mold design.

Sheet Metal Fabrication tolerances depend on bending and cutting accuracy and typically range from ±0.1 mm to ±0.5 mm.

Tighter tolerances increase manufacturing cost and inspection requirements.
