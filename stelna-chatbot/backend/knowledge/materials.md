## Aluminum 6061-T6

Aluminum 6061-T6 offers a high strength-to-weight ratio and good corrosion resistance. It is widely used for structural components, brackets, and enclosures.

It is easy to machine and suitable for CNC machining. It provides good dimensional stability and moderate cost.


## Mild Steel

Mild steel provides high structural strength at low material cost. It is commonly used for industrial frames and load-bearing components.

However, it is heavier than aluminum and requires surface treatment to prevent corrosion.


## Stainless Steel

Stainless steel provides excellent corrosion resistance and high durability. It is suitable for harsh environments and industrial applications.

It is heavier and more expensive than mild steel and aluminum.


## Carbon-Fiber-Reinforced Nylon

Carbon-fiber-reinforced nylon provides high stiffness and low weight. It is commonly used in functional prototypes and lightweight structural components.

It is suitable for advanced 3D printing and provides improved strength compared to standard nylon.


## ABS

ABS is a commonly used thermoplastic with good impact resistance and moderate strength. It is widely used in injection molding and vacuum forming.

It provides good surface finish and is suitable for enclosures and consumer products.
