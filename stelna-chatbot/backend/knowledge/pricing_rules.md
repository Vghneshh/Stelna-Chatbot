## Manufacturing Pricing Factors

Manufacturing pricing depends on several variables:

Material type influences raw material cost and machining difficulty.

Part size affects material consumption and machine time.

Quantity significantly impacts cost efficiency. Tooling-based processes such as injection molding reduce per-part cost at high volumes.

Complex geometry increases machining time and programming effort.

Surface finish requirements add secondary operations and cost.

Tolerance requirements increase inspection and machining precision costs.

Tooling cost applies to processes such as injection molding and vacuum forming.
